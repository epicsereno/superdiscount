Common Questions

Everything neighbors want to know

Do you accept EBT?

Yes! We accept EBT (SNAP) for all eligible grocery and snack items. We are proud to be EBT-friendly for the El Sereno community.

Do you deliver rental tables and chairs?

Currently, we offer local pickup for all party rentals. We recommend a truck or large SUV for table sets. Call us to confirm availability before you stop by!

Is there parking available?

There is limited street parking directly in front of the store on Eastern Ave and additional parking on the surrounding side streets.

Can I check if something is in stock?

Absolutely. Our inventory moves fast, so feel free to call us at (323) 223-8115 and we'll check the aisles for you immediately.
